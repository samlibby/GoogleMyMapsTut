Directions from Cape Town to West Coast National Park, South Africa

using: https://maps.googleapis.com/maps/api/directions/json?origin=Cape+Town+South+Africa&destination=West+Coast+National+Park&avoid=highways&departure_time=now&key=[API_KEY]

The parameters used are: Origin = Cape Town, South Africa, Destination = West Coast National Park, Avoid = Highways, Departure Time = Now
